Welcome

Defence against the dark text arts - NLP approaches to understanding and defending against cyber threats (fake news, cyber safety and more)

This class focuses on Natural Language Processing through the lens of a better understanding of how we can protect ourselves from internet based safety threats. You will fight fake news, hate speech, and identify deceptive persons online.

Note: This is not an official course website. This is an informational site to share some resources for other people who might be interested in resources and research in this area. For UP students who have enrolled on this class information should be accessed through ClickUP!!!
