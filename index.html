About the Course

This course introduces the fundamentals of Natural Language Processing (NLP) with a focus on real-world applications in data science. Topics include text representation, language modeling, embeddings, transformers, classification, named entity recognition, and more. Students engage in hands-on projects and collaborative assignments using South African and other low-resource language data.
